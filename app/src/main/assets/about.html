Explanation

To use this app, you have to enter the verbs corresponding with the person and the verb displayed.

If the answer is correct you win one point, if it is incorrect you loose one point and is added to the mistakes list.

Pressing over a mistake lets you try again. The app stores the maximum points and the mistakes.
